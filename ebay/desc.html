About Us


We specialize in the Dell PowerEdge and PowerVault series as well as HP’s ProLiant and StorageWorks product line. EZ Trade Live provides businesses with the quality IT equipment they require at affordable prices.

EZ Trade Live operates out of a 20,000 square foot climate controlled warehouse. View the images of our 5,000+ in-stock servers, workstations, and storage arrays.

We take pride in our Atlanta based showroom with many combinations of Dell and HP, rack and tower servers. We have on-site Dell and HP qualified technicians that work in a fully functional testing environment. The technicians process the order and run full diagnostics to test every server before the product is sent out to our valued customers. On staff IT help is only a call away for any troubleshooting or warranty issues.
